Vanilla Buttercream Frosting

Time: 10 minutes

Yields: 24 servings

Recipe Link: Vanilla Buttercream Frosting

Special Ingredients:

1 stick butter, softened
5-6 Tbsp milk

Notes:

  • If you replace the butter with 1 cup Blue Bonnet, the frosting will be smoother, and you won't have to add milk. Due to misreading the recipe, 1 cup was used instead of 1/2 cup.
  • If you use regular butter, you may want to use slightly more than 6 Tbsp milk.
