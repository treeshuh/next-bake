Chocolate Cake

Time: ~1 hr

Yields: 12 servings

Recipe Link: Chocolate Cake

Special Ingredients:

3/4 cup cocoa
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Notes:

  • This cake is super moist and soft and not too chocolate-y.
  • (Optional) Add chocolate chips after stirring in the boiling water.
  • Use 3/8 cup regular cocoa powder and 3/8 cup Swiss Miss milk chocolate cocoa mix
